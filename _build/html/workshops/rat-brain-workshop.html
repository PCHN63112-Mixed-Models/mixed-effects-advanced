Alternative Models#

The clearest alternative we could work with here would be to integrate region into the model, rather than treating it as simply an index of replications. This would allow us to assess whether the brain region itself differs in the measurement of …, as well as entertaining the possibility of a treatment:region interaction, assessing whether the effect of the treatment changes depending upon the region of the brain.
